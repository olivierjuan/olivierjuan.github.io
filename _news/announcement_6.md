Paul Strang, PhD student in our team, received an **Honorable Mention** at the [MIP Workshop 2023 Computational Competition (MIPcc23)](https://github.com/ambros-gleixner/MIPcc23) held at the University of Southern California!

MIPcc23, organized on the occasion of the **20th anniversary of the Mixed Integer Programming Workshop**, challenged participants to solve Mixed Integer Programs efficiently under reoptimization — i.e., after slight changes to input data. Our team proposed **Influence Branching**, a graph-oriented variable selection strategy combined with multi-armed bandit hyperparameter tuning (Thompson sampling) to learn to solve MIPs online.

**Authors:** Paul Strang, Zacharie Alès, Côme Bissuel, Olivier Juan, Safia Kedad-Sidhoum, Emmanuel Rachelson
